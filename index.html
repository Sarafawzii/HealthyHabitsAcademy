Hydration & Sleep

Proper hydration and sleep are essential pillars of a healthy lifestyle. Dehydration can affect your mood, focus, and energy, while good sleep helps with recovery, memory, and fat burning. Aim to drink at least 8 cups of water daily and get 7-9 hours of quality sleep per night.

Hydration Tip

  • Start your morning with a glass of water to activate your metabolism.

Sleep Tip

  • Avoid screen time at least 30 minutes before bedtime to improve sleep quality.
